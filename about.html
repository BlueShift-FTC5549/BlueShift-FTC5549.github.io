BLUE SHIFT

ABOUT

Asheville School Blues Robotics was born in October 2011 as a brand new team trying to make an innovation. It is a completely student-run organization, made up of 12 student members and three faculty advisors. We are hosting the qualifying round at Asheville School in hopes of moving on in the competition.

The main purpose of this blog is to spread out the exciting news of our team every day. We will post everything such as every meeting, every progress, every photo, every achievement etc.

Team members and new posts can be found easily on the other pages. Feel free to leave any concernand suggestion. You can leave a comment or send an email to us.

Special Thanks to:

Mr. Long & The Asheville School Community
